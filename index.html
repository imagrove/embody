`Imagrove Embody` is a decentralized open-source platform for **Embodied Hardware**. We bridge the gap between digital code and atomic reality through a self-evolving manufacturing cell.

Five Dimensions of Embody

01

Embodied Intelligence

Translating high-level design intent directly into precise motion and logical code. The machine that understands material constraints.

02

Hybrid Synthesis

Multi-material integration. Synthesizing complex products—from additive shells to PCBA insertion—within a single unit.

03

Logic Loop

Closing the loop between hardware and software. Building the body while simultaneously flashing and validating the soul (code).

04

Self-Evolution

The Mother Machine. Embody is designed to manufacture its own replacement parts and advanced actuators. Iteration without limits.

05

Project Iteration

Continuous hardware evolution. V1.0 to V5.0 happens in a seamless, automated flow within the same machine.

Open Source. Open Future.

Hardware is the last frontier of the open-source revolution. We are releasing the core designs, scheduling logic, and evolutionary blueprints of `Embody` to the world.
